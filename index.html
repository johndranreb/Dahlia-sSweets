Our History

  • August 1993

    Dahlia's Sweets, a beloved confectionery paradise, began its delightful journey in 1993. Founded with a passion for creating mouthwatering treats, Dahlia's Sweets quickly captured the hearts of dessert enthusiasts far and wide.

  • 1998

    In the year 1998, Dahlia's Sweets expanded its reach and opened a branch in sunny California, USA.

  • 2010

    In the year 2010, Dahlia's Sweets embarked on an exciting international expansion, opening branches in the Philippines, Spain, Australia, and Dubai.

  • Present

    As of the present day, Dahlia's Sweets has successfully established itself with an impressive presence, boasting 22 branches worldwide. From its humble beginnings in 1993 to its current global reach, Dahlia's Sweets continues to bring joy and delight to sweet lovers across the globe.
